Projects

Meerkat is a protoype for a webapp that I created with three other students at HackDavis 2016, the first major collegiate hackathon at UC Davis. We wanted to make a webapp with a goal of improving communication between students and surpass current webapps that we used for school. With Meerkat, students can form study groups, manage lengthy group projects, upload their own class resources, and chat with classmates.
